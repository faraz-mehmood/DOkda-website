Who We Are

بِسْمِ اللَّهِ الرَّحْمَنِ الرَّحِيمِ

In the Name of Allah, the Most Gracious, the Most Merciful

Alhamdulillāh, for more than 10 years, DOKDA (Deutsch Orientalisch Kulturverein Darmstadt), has been serving our diverse Muslim community — Pakistani, Indian, Bangladeshi, and Muslims from all backgrounds — with dedication and love.

Over the years, DOKDA has organised daily prayers, Ramadan iftars, Eid gatherings, and Islamic classes for children, youth, and adults. We also help new students arriving in Darmstadt and nearby areas by supporting them with accommodation and helping them settle into the community so they feel at home.

With Allah’s blessings and your continued support, we have reached a huge milestone — we have purchased our own worship and community facility in Darmstadt!

🆕 Final Payment Appeal — €50,000

We still need €50,000 to complete the final payment on our building. Every contribution brings us one step closer to securing a permanent home for our community.

May Allah reward you abundantly — Sadaqah Jariyah for every prayer held in this space.

Our Mission

To serve as a spiritual home for Muslims in Darmstadt — fostering connection to Allah, to our faith, and to one another — while building bridges with the broader German community.
