Why Your Donation Matters

DOKDA relies entirely on the generosity of our community. Your donations fund the day-to-day running of the mosque, our educational programmes, community events, and maintenance of the building.

"The example of those who spend their wealth in the way of Allah is like a seed of grain which grows seven spikes; in each spike is a hundred grains."
— Quran 2:261

Whether it's a regular standing order or a one-off gift, every contribution makes a difference. May Allah accept from you and reward you abundantly.

What Your Donation Supports

  • Daily operations & utilities
  • Quran and Islamic education classes
  • Youth and children's programmes
  • Building maintenance and improvements
  • Community events and iftars
  • Zakat and Sadaqah distribution to those in need
