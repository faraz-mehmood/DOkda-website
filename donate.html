Why Your Donation Matters

DOKDA relies entirely on the generosity of our community. Your donations fund the day-to-day running of the mosque, our educational programmes, community events, and maintenance of the building.

Whether it's a regular standing order or a one-off gift, every contribution makes a difference. May Allah accept from you and reward you abundantly.
